Download Kaleidoscope Project Applications

As it was mentioned in other documents, Kaleidoscope project has two Java desktop applications: "The Qualitative Dynamical Systems Modeling & Simulation Environment" and "The Space Exploration Mission, Agent-Robot Behavior Simulator" created for supporting development the McLN models and suggest to download and use them.

Both applications are distributed in the form of their installers assembled for macOS, Windows and Linux computers. They can be downloaded from this page and placed on the hard disk in accordance with the standards of named OSs. Installers having extension .pkg are intended for installing and executing applications on the computers equipped with macOS. Installers having extension .msi are intended for installing and running applications on the Windows computers. And installers having extension .exe are intended for installing and running applications on the Linux computers.

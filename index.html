Kaleidoscope Project
(Qualitative Systems, Modeling Technique and Formalisms)

Kaleidoscope Project is a pilot research and development initiative intended to introduce notion of the Qualitative Dynamical Systems and to explore feasibility of mathematical modeling of behavior of the qualitative dynamical systems. The Qualitative Dynamical Systems, the Qualitative Dynamical Systems Behavior Mathematical Modeling Technique, and the Modeling Solutions proposed by the Kaleidoscope Project are described on the cluster of the webpages referred to in the course of description of the Project.

Page: Qualitative Dynamical Systems, Their
Behavior and Mathematical Modeling

Objective of this page is to introduce notion of class of Qualitative Dynamical Systems and to describe characteristics of the nature and behavior of Qualitative Dynamical Systems, constituting foundation for creation of mathematical models of these systems. This page identifies atomic element of behavior of the systems and describes basic ways of composition of these elements letting the models of the system to be capable of reproducing various forms of behavior, observed in the qualitative dynamical systems.

Qualitative Dynamical Systems Modeling
Technique's Modeling Solutions

There are three major modeling solutions based on the Algebra of Symbols. They are: 1) "Extended Disjunctive Normal Form (XDNF)", 2) "Model's Vector State-Space Representation", and 3) "Multi-colored Logical Net Graphical Modeling Formalism".

Solution 1: Extended Disjunctive Normal Form - XDNF,
and XDNF Expression Calculator

Page, titled: "Extended Disjunctive Normal Form of the Algebraic Expression and the Expression Calculator", illustrates a special case. It describes dependence of states of a single qualitative variable on the state of several groups of qualitative variables. This dependence is computed by a qualitative function, implemented in the form of algebraic expression written as the composition of operations of the Algebra of Symbols, structure of which is called Extended Disjunctive Normal Form (XDNF). Unlike the well known DNF, XDNF includes not only operations with such familiar names as Equivalence, Conjunction and Disjunction (which are also present in the Algebra of Symbol), but, in addition, has two more operations named: Production, and Application. Meaning and roles of all five operations is described on this page. Open the Page

Solution 2: Vector State-Space Model Representation and Model Transitions Calculator

This page is titled: "Qualitative Dynamical Systems Vector State-Space Modeling Approach and the Models Matrix Equations Calculator". It describes qualitative dynamical systems mathematical modeling technique based on the representation of these systems in the form of vector state matrix equations of the systems. In simple models and the step-by step style of computation, the page illustrates mechanism of simulation of behavior of the modeled qualitative systems, which is carried out as the sequence of transformations of states of the models and visualization of the results of the computation. Desktop Applications Developed and Used
in the Kaleidoscope Project

In addition to the pages that introduced: "Qualitative Dynamical Systems", "Qualitative Dynamical Systems Behavior Modeling Technique" and the pages described modeling solutions, suggested by the Technique, two following pages provide a number of screenshots illustrating different views of the software tools developed in the Kaleidoscope project for supporting creation and simulation of the McLN models.

Page, titled: "Qualitative Dynamical Systems Modeling & Simulation Environment", that is named after the application describes on it, presents several snapshots of the UI of the application. This application is a development tool consisting of: 1) Graphical Creator/Editor - place where a user can create the McLN model, and 2) the McLN Model Simulator - area that lets the user to: start, stop, or execute the model step-by-step, as well as to reset the model into its initial state.

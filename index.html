Kaleidoscope Project
(Qualitative Systems, Modeling Technique and Formalisms)

Kaleidoscope Project is a pilot research and development initiative intended to introduce notion of the Qualitative Dynamical Systems and to explore feasibility of mathematical modeling of behavior of the qualitative dynamical systems. The Qualitative Dynamical Systems, the Qualitative Dynamical Systems Behavior Mathematical Modeling Technique, and the Modeling Solutions proposed by the Kaleidoscope Project are described on the cluster of the webpages referred to in the course of description of the Project.

Page: Qualitative Dynamical Systems, Their
Behavior and Mathematical Modeling

Objective of this page is to introduce a notion of the class of Qualitative Dynamical System and to explore characteristics of the nature and the behavior of Qualitative Systems, constituting the basis for creation of mathematical models of these systems. This page identifies atomic element of the behavior of the systems and describes the ways of composition of the elements letting the model of a system to be able to reproduce various forms of behavior observed in the Qualitative Dynamical Systems being modeled. Page: Qualitative Dynamical Systems Behavior
Mathematical Modeling Technique

The page introduces qualitative systems modeling solutions found by the Kaleidoscope Project in the course of exploration of feasibility of studying qualitative systems by means of developing models and simulation of behavior of these systems.
As the behavior of dynamical systems is generally perceived as a sequence of observed transitions of states of properties of the systems over time, modeling solutions suggest:
a) defines a model as a collection of variables that represent qualitative properties of modeled system and therefore are supposed to take qualitative values; b) suggests mechanism of computation of changes of values of the variables in the form of state function associated with every variable; and providing an algebraic system elements of carrier set of which could be used to represent qualitative values of the variables, while the operations defined on the set should be used for carrying out steps of computation of the functions. Open the Page

This section: a) describes technique of computation of a variable state function that lays down requirements to algebraic operations needed for computation of qualitative functions; b) introduces the system of algebraic operations based on the established requirements carrier set of which is the set of symbols given as the letters of the Latin alphabet, and finally c) illustrates application of the algebra-based qualitative systems modeling technique by presenting several examples of interactive computation of qualitative functions written in the form of XDNF expressions.

Qualitative Dynamical Systems Modeling
Technique's Modeling Solutions

There are three major modeling solutions based on the Algebra of symbols developed in the Kaleidoscope project: 1) "Extended Disjunctive Normal Form (XDNF)", 2) "Vector State Space Modeling Technique", and 3) "Multi-colored Logical Net Graphical Modeling Formalism".

Solution 1: Extended Disjunctive Normal Form and
XDNF-Expression Calculator

The page, titled: "Extended Disjunctive Normal Form Expression Calculator", illustrates a special case of describing the dependence of state of one qualitative variable on the state of a group of qualitative variables. This dependence is described as a qualitative function, expression of which is defined in the form of Extended Disjunctive Normal Form (XDNF). Unlike simple DNF, XDNF includes not only familiar operations Conjunction and Disjunction which are also present in the Algebra of Symbol, but also three other operations of the Algebra called: Equivalence, Production, and Application. Meaning of these operations is described on the proposed page. Open the Page

Equation and the Equation Calculator

The page, titled: "Qualitative Dynamical Systems Vector State-Space Models Simulation Calculator", illustrates mathematical modeling technique based on the representation of qualitative dynamical systems in the form of vector state equation of the systems. Modeling Formalism

The page, titled: "Qualitative Dynamical Systems Multi-Colored Logical Net Graphical Modeling Formalism", outlines the essence of the questions arising at the time of developing mathematical models of Qualitative Dynamical Systems.
